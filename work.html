Custom Website Development

Are you a small business owner or new online service provider looking for someone to help you develop your dream website? Are looking for a change in your online presence, or for someone to relocate or recreate your existing website?

I have experience developing responsive websites from scratch, as well as using popular platforms, such as WordPress, Wix, and Kajabi.

I aim to build a website that is right for you and your business by optimizing your website's performance and ensuring it is user friendly.

Each website calls for different specifications depending on your business's wants and needs. A Basic Website Package starts at as little as $500. Every website build includes*:

  • 4 standard webpages (homepage, about, services/blog, contact)
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Consultation about the best services for hosting your website
  • Basic SEO, security, backups, and load speed optimization
  • Email set-up assistance
  • Progress check-ins and updates throughout the build
  • A recorded video run-down of your website, maintenance, and how to make simple changes after the build is complete

*Services and cost varies depending on platform, hosting, and website specifications

Contact me for a consultation about your website, and we can discuss next steps toward building a website that best meets your business's needs.

Branding, website copy and images, and e-commerce pages are not included in the basic website package, but I am happy to discuss the posibilities for adding these or other features to your website build package!
